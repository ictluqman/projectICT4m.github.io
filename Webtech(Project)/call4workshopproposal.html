Call for Workshop Proposals

In conjunction with ICT4M2018, the organizers would like to call for workshops proposals. Proposed workshops should be in line with

the conference theme. Workshop should provide opportunities for researchers and practitioners to have in-depth discussions on

particular topics of interests. Workshops may be focused on any topics related to the conference theme, including:


- Industrial 4.0 (Internet of Things)

- Research and Analytic Tools

- Financial Technologies (FinTech)

- Data Science

- Big Data Analytics & Visualization

- Intellectual Properties (Patent Searching and Drafting)

- Research & Analytic Tools
